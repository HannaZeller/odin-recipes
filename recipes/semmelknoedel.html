Semmelknödel

Description

Semmelknödel are amazing and one of my favourite foods (I have great taste).
They use old Semmeln (bread rolls) that are too old and dry to be enjoyed anymore and make them into a fabulous meal.
They can be a side dish to meat and a nice sauce, but they can also be the star of the whole meal in a nice creamy mushroom sauce for example.
Even on their own, cut into slices and pan fried in butter, they are absolutely delicious!
